For My Beautiful Alina

Alina,

I made a mistake — one that’s left me aching with regret.
You deserve honesty, and I failed you. But you also deserve love — and mine for you is deep, real, and endless.

I miss your laugh, your eyes, your voice. I miss us.

I think about all the little things: our dates, the way you hold my hand, the way time stops when you smile at me.
I love those moments — I love you.

I know I hurt you. I’m so sorry. And I know I can’t take back what I did, but I’ll spend every day proving I can be better — for you, for us.

I can’t lose you, Alina. You’re everything to me. I see a future with you.
We’ll get through this. And one day, we’ll look back at this as something that made us stronger — because I believe with all my heart that we’ll get married, build a life, and grow old together.

Please forgive me.
I love you always.

Forever yours,
The one who loves you endlessly
